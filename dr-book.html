Dieter Rams Book

design

Dieter Rams has been one of my design idols (and the idol of many other designers) for quite some time. When I was tasked with creating a book exemplifying design principals that influenced and inspired my work, I naturally went straight to Rams’ 10 Principals For Good Design.

While designing the book, I adhered to Dieter’s principals, maintaining an unobtrusive, minimalistic, and aesthetically pleasing design. I used a simple grid system for the book, based on the dimensions of the Braun T3 Pocket Radio - the product known for influencing the design of Apple’s iPod.

The products showcased in the book span Dieter’s work for both Braun and Vitsoe.
